Process

Knowing what to expect when we work together is crucial – here is an overview to my approach and how I ensure a successful delivery.

Consultation

It all starts with a conversation. We'll coordinate time to discuss the details of your project, learn more about your current needs, and talk through solutions tailored for your business.

Proposal

A detailed project proposal will be presented, including visual aids and a plan of action. Each suggestion will list a quote and full breakdown. We can fine tune the details and collaborate on finding the best course of action.

Creation

After approval and kickoff, clients will receive regular updates on progress and test features. Communication and trust is a top priority when working through the project timeline.

Launch & Support

Peace of mind is delivered with the completed project in the form of support.
